Food Menu

Idli & Sambar

Idli and Sambar is a classic South Indian breakfast dish. Together, Idli and Sambar offer a delicious and satisfying meal that is both healthy and comforting.

Price: ₹ 60

Dosa

Dosa is a thin, crispy, and savory crepe-like pancake originating from South India. It is made from a fermented batter of ground black gram (urad dal) and rice.

Price: ₹ 80

Filter Coffee

Filter coffee is a South Indian brewing method that produces a strong, aromatic, and deeply flavorful coffee. Traditionally prepared in a metal filter, it involves layering roasted and ground coffee with sugar, and slowly dripping hot water through it. The result is a concentrated, intensely flavored coffee that is often enjoyed black, but can also be enjoyed with milk and sugar

Price: ₹ 20

Poori & Saagu

Poori is a deep-fried Indian flatbread made from whole wheat flour. It is typically round and puffy, and it has a crisp and flaky texture. Poori is often served hot and fresh, and it is best enjoyed when it is still warm.

Price: ₹ 50

Butter-Non & Paneer masala

Indulge in the richness of our creamy and flavorful butter non. Made with fresh milk and churned to perfection, this non is a true treat for the senses. Its smooth texture and subtle sweetness make it the perfect accompaniment to any meal. Our Paneer Masala is a classic Indian dish that combines the softness of paneer (Indian cottage cheese) with a rich and aromatic gravy. The paneer is cooked in a creamy tomato-based sauce infused with a blend of Indian spices, creating a symphony of flavors that will tantalize your taste buds.

Price: ₹ 180

Hydrabadi Biriyani

Hyderabadi Biryani is a renowned dish from Hyderabad, India, celebrated for its rich flavors and aromatic spices. It's a layered rice dish typically made with fragrant basmati rice, succulent meat (often mutton or chicken), and a medley of spices like saffron, cardamom, cloves, and cinnamon. The meat is often marinated overnight in yogurt and spices, then cooked with the rice and sealed in a pot to allow the flavors to meld together.

Price: ₹ 150
